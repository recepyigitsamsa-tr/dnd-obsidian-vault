## Traits

**Death Throes.** Camlash explodes when reduced to 0 hit points, and each creature within 30 feet of Camlash must make a DC 21 Saving Throw Dexterity saving throw, taking 70 (20d6 damage) fire damage on a failed save or half as much damage on a successful one. The explosion ignites flammable objects in that area that aren't being worn or carried.

**Magic Resistance.** Camlash has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

**Spider Aura.** Camlash is surrounded by tiny biting spiders that magically appear and disappear from moment to moment. At the start of each of Camlash's turns, each creature within 10 feet of Camlash takes 10 (3d6 damage) poison damage and must succeed on a DC 21 Saving Throw Constitution saving throw or have the paralyzed condition condition until the start of Camlash's next turn.
