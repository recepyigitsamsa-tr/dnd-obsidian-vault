**Multiattack.** The inquisitor attacks twice with its Silver Longsword or uses Mind Fire twice.

**Silver Longsword.** {@atk mw} +7 to hit to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. {@h}8 (1d8 + 4 damage) slashing damage, or 9 ({@dice 1d10 + 4}) if used with two hands, plus 18 (4d8 damage) force damage.

**Mind Fire.** The inquisitor targets one creature it can see within 120 feet of it. The target must succeed on a DC 15 Saving Throw Intelligence saving throw or take 17 (3d8 + 4 damage) psychic damage and be stunned condition until the start of the inquisitor's next turn.

**Inquisitor's Command {@recharge 5}.** Each creature of the inquisitor's choice that it can see within 60 feet of it must succeed on a DC 15 Saving Throw Wisdom saving throw or be charmed condition until the start of the inquisitor's next turn. On the charmed condition target's turn, the inquisitor can telepathically control the target's move, action, or both. When controlled in this way, the target can take only the Attack (inquisitor chooses the target) or Dash action.
