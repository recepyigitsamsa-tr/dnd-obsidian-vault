## Description

You gain a +3 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls made with this sword. In addition, while you hold the sword, you can take a {@variantrule Reaction|XPHB} to make one melee attack with it against any creature in your reach that deals damage to you. You have {@variantrule Advantage|XPHB} on the attack roll, and any damage dealt with this special attack ignores any {@variantrule Immunity|XPHB} or {@variantrule Resistance|XPHB} the target has to that damage.
