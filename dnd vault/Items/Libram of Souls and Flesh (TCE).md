## Description

With covers made of skin and fittings of bone, this tome is cold to the touch, and it whispers faintly. When found, the book contains the following spells, which are wizard spells for you while you are attuned to the book: animate dead spell, circle of death spell, false life spell, finger of death spell, speak with dead spell, summon undead|TCE spell, and vampiric touch spell. It functions as a spellbook for you.

While you are holding the book, you can use it as a spellcasting focus for your wizard spells.
